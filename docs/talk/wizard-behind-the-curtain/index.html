Wizard Behind the Curtain

Abstract

Come and listen to the presentation, ``Real Introduction to Block-chain”. New technologies often create a lack of mistrust and understanding which makes way for those wishing to take advantage. Fraser Bullock, from the team at ThinkHub.io, will present an insight into Block-chain. The talk will assist people to better understand this technology, in order to ensure you can make informed decisions, which ultimately provides you with the right outcome. So come along, enjoy the great food and gain some valuable, and very timely, information.
